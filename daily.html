Daily Challenge for You

1 - Aayat al-Kursiy

(Al-Baqarahh:255)

Abu Hurayrah (may Allaah be pleased with him) said: The Messenger of Allaah (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) said: “When you go to your bed, recite Aayat al-Kursiy, for there will remain with you a guardian from Allaah, and no devil will approach you, until morning comes.” Sahih Al Bukhari (2311).

2 - Last two verses of Baqarah

(Al-Baqarahh:285,286)

Abu Mas'ood al-Ansaari (may Allaah be pleased with him) that the Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) said: “Whoever recites the last two verses of Soorat al-Baqarah at night, they will suffice him.” Sahih Al Bukhari (5009) and Muslim (2714).

3, 4 - Soorat al-Isra' and Soorat al-Zumar

(Chapter No:17,39)

It was narrated that 'Aa'ishah (may Allaah be pleased with her) said: The Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) would not go to sleep until he had recited Bani Israa'eel (Soorat al-Isra') and al-Zumar. Al-Tirmidhi (3402)

5 - Soorat al-Kaafiroon

(Chapter No:109)

It was narrated that Nawfal al-Ashja'i (may Allaah be pleased with him) said: The Messenger of Allaah (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) said to me: “Recite Qul yaa ayyuha'l-kaafiroon then go to sleep at the end of it, for it is a disavowal of shirk.” Abu Dawood (5055) and al-Tirmidhi (3400)

6, 7, 8 - al-Ikhlaas and al-Mi'wadhatayn

[i.e., the last three soorahs of the Qur'aan]

(Chapter No:112,113,114)

It was narrated from 'Aa'ishah (may Allaah be pleased with her) that when the Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) went to bed every night, he would hold his hands together and blow into them, and recite into them Qul Huwa Allaahu Ahad, Qul a'oodhu bi rabb il-falaq and Qul a'oodhu bi rabb il-naas. Then he would wipe them over whatever he could of his body, starting with his head and face and the front of his body, and he would do that three times. al-Bukhaari (5017).

9 - Surah Mulk

(Chapter No:67)

It was narrated from Abu Hurayrah that the Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) said: “There is a soorah of the Qur'aan containing thirty verses which have interceded for a man until he was forgiven. It is the soorah Tabaarak alladhi bi yadihi'l-mulk.” al-Tirmidhi, 2891

10 - Surah Sajdah

(Chapter No:32)

Narrated from Jaabir (may Allaah be pleased with him) that the Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) did not sleep until he had recited “Alif‑Laam‑Meem. The revelation…” [al-Sajdah 32] and “Blessed is He in whose hand is dominion…” [al- Mulk 67]. Al-Tirmidhi (2892)
