QURAN

Quran is the holy book of Muslims, revealed to the last messenger, Prophet Mohammed ﷺ (PBUH).It was revealed by the Allah to the Prophet Muhammed ﷺ via the angel Jibrael in the west Arabian towns Mecca and Medina. The Quran was revealed over a period of around 23 years, beginning in the month of Ramadhan, when Prophet Muhammed ﷺ was 40 years old.It comprises 30 Juzs and 114 Chapters.Each sūrah consists of several verses, known as āyāt, which originally means a 'sign' or 'evidence' sent by Allah. Chapters are classified as Meccan or Medinan, depending on whether the verses were revealed before or after the migration of Prophet Muhammed ﷺ to the city of Medina. For Muslims, the Quran is the word of Allah and contains complete guidance for Mankind.
